# Third-Party Notes

The semantic lint additions first introduced in version 3.4.0 and extended in version 3.5.0 were informed by the public `hardikpandya/stop-slop` repository.

Useful concepts were adapted semantically:

- false agency
- narrator-from-a-distance voice
- rhetorical setup removal
- permission-granting ending removal
- density and cuttability checks
- rhythm checks
- before/after examples

The Personal Humanizer implementation intentionally does not adopt that project’s universal bans on all adverbs, passive voice, Wh-word openings, em dashes, or three-item lists.

Repository: https://github.com/hardikpandya/stop-slop
License listed by the repository: MIT.


Version 3.5.0 extends the approach with original standalone-post and long-form structural checks, including paragraph-job mapping, recap-transition detection, repeated paragraph arcs, abstraction creep, and conclusion necessity.
